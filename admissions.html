Admissions at NSRIT

Welcome to NSRIT! We are committed to providing a transparent and streamlined admission process for students seeking to join our prestigious institution. Below are the details of the admission process for various programs.

Eligibility Criteria

  • B.Tech: Candidates must have passed the 10+2 examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as main subjects. A valid score in AP EAMCET (Engineering Agricultural and Medical Common Entrance Test) is mandatory.
  • M.Tech: Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree in the relevant discipline with a valid score in GATE or AP PGECET.
  • MBA: Candidates must have a Bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50% marks. A valid score in AP ICET (Integrated Common Entrance Test) is required.
  • Lateral Entry: Diploma holders are eligible for lateral entry into the second year of B.Tech through the AP ECET (Engineering Common Entrance Test).

Admission Procedure

  1. Step 1: Appear for the respective entrance examination (AP EAMCET, AP PGECET, GATE, or AP ICET).
  2. Step 2: Participate in the state-level counseling process conducted by APSCHE (Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education).
  3. Step 3: Choose NSRIT as your preferred institution during counseling.
  4. Step 4: After seat allotment, report to the NSRIT admissions office for document verification and fee payment.
  5. Step 5: Complete the admission formalities and secure your place at NSRIT.

Documents Required

  • 10th and 12th mark sheets
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Conduct Certificate
  • Category Certificate (if applicable)
  • AP EAMCET/AP PGECET/GATE/AP ICET Rank Card
  • Aadhar Card
  • Passport-sized Photographs

Fee Structure

  • B.Tech: ₹75,000 per year (subject to change as per APSCHE guidelines)
  • M.Tech: ₹90,000 per year
  • MBA: ₹70,000 per year

Important Dates (2025-2026)

  • AP EAMCET Exam Date: May 10, 2025
  • AP PGECET Exam Date: June 5, 2025
  • AP ICET Exam Date: May 20, 2025
  • Commencement of Classes: July 15, 2025
