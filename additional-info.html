Insights

NSRIT stands out as a premier institution providing technical excellence in education.With a strong emphasis on research, innovation, and academic rigor, NSRIT consistently ranks among the top technical institutes in India. The institute fosters a culture of learning and leadership, preparing students to excel in a competitive global environment.

Exam Cell

The Exam Cell at NSRIT is responsible for conducting smooth and transparent examinations. Key responsibilities include:

  • Coordinating internal and external exams.
  • Ensuring strict adherence to exam guidelines.
  • Facilitating student exam-related queries.

Placements

NSRIT has a dedicated placement cell that works closely with top-tier companies to provide students with excellent job opportunities. Recent highlights include:

  • Average package: ₹6 LPA
  • Highest package: ₹20 LPA
  • Top recruiters: Infosys, TCS, Wipro, Microsoft, and Amazon
  • Over 90% placement rate for eligible students
